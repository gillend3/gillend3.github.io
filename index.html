Getting Into The Flow

Sometimes, when we are writing, we get into our own heads. Was that really the right word to use? Did I
spell it right? Maybe I should check the thesaurus for a better one. All of this fiddling and fussing takes
you out of that flow state which is so important to writing – particularly the first draft, or what Adam calls
the “mud-slinging” phase. This app is designed to help you enter and maintain a deep flow state, that magical
place where the subconscious comes alive and produces wonders.

Instructions: It's a simple app: you just type in the box. The only catch is this - no deletions, no corrections.
Just keep moving forward.
