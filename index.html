Hemingway Drafting

According to a story I read on the Internet (almost certainly apocryphal), Hemingway used to write
his drafts in a sweep of forward motion - no looking back, no corrections - until he had achieved
what he had set out to. With this app, you can't delete any of your text: you have to keep moving
forward!

Instructions: This tool is designed to try and get you into a flow state - no deletions, no corrections.
Just keep moving forward. You can choose to type in easy mode (no deletions allowed) or hard
mode (you can have up to 10 deletions, but if you go over that limit, all of your text gets deleted.)
